NB · CCTVInitiative 06 is intentionally narrow: vision systems at hazard zones and PPE checkpoints, not broad workplace surveillance. Australian state-based workplace surveillance legislation (NSW Workplace Surveillance Act 2005, VIC/QLD/WA equivalents) requires written notice, written policy, and EBA consultation where applicable. Combining surveillance with role-displacing automation is a textbook industrial-relations trigger and undermines the redeployment narrative that protects RMF grant eligibility. The genuine traceability outcome — knowing which feedstock, shift, run, and operator produced any 1000kg jumbo bag — is delivered by Initiatives 01 and 03 (MES + LIMS + digital work instructions), not by cameras pointed at workers.
